Arriving in Nairobi



We had barely arrived in Nairobi when we discovered a few differences from our last stay on this beautiful continent. 1) Nairobi has skyscrapers (we're not living in the big village anymore) 2) we need to learn how to negotiate with cab drivers in Swahili and 3) Leigh's potential to beat Matt on the squash court will be greatly increased by the proximity of the courts to our new temporary apartment.
